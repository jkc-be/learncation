About Learnoliday

What is Learnoliday?

Learnoliday provides immersive English learning experiences for children through carefully designed holiday programs. We combine structured language education with engaging activities, creating an environment where children naturally improve their English while having fun.

Benefits of English Immersion

  • Natural language acquisition through daily use
  • Increased confidence in speaking English
  • Cultural awareness and global perspective
  • Academic advantages and improved communication skills
  • Memorable experiences with international peers

Age Groups

Our programs are designed for children aged 8-17 years, with age-appropriate activities and learning objectives for each group.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you accept?

We accept children aged 8-17 years. Programs are divided into age-appropriate groups to ensure optimal learning and social experiences.

What is the teacher-to-student ratio?

We maintain a maximum ratio of 1 teacher to 12 students in classes, and 1 supervisor to 8 students during activities, ensuring personalized attention and safety.

What English level is required?

We welcome children of all English levels, from beginners to advanced. We conduct placement assessments to ensure each child is in the appropriate class.

Where are the programs held?

Our programs are held in Belgium, in a safe and beautiful location with modern facilities and easy access to cultural sites.

What is included in the program fee?

The fee includes English lessons, accommodation, all meals, activities, excursions, supervision, and course materials. Airport transfers can be arranged for an additional fee.
