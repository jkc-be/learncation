About Learncation

Where Learning Meets Adventure

Our Story

Learncation combines Learn + Vacation to create unforgettable English learning experiences for young learners across Asia.

Our Hong Kong-based immersive camps let kids practice English through cultural tours, teamwork activities, and exciting games in one of Asia's most vibrant cities.

Can't travel? Join our fun online bootcamps with live, interactive lessons led by engaging native English teachers from anywhere in the world!

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower young learners with English language skills through immersive, adventure-based experiences that combine education with cultural exploration.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of experiential English education in Asia, where every child discovers the joy of learning through meaningful adventures and global connections.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We maintain the highest standards in teaching and program delivery
  • Adventure: Learning should be exciting, engaging, and memorable
  • Safety: Your child's wellbeing is our absolute priority
  • Inclusivity: We welcome learners of all levels and backgrounds
  • Cultural Exchange: We celebrate diversity and foster global understanding
